The Semantic Content Management.

Alfresco module that enables the semantic analysis of documents and extraction of entities such as persons, organizations, places, etc.

The module consists in two components:

  • Alfresco component: Contains all the components to enhance a document and store the extracted information.
  • Share component: Shows the extracted information for each document and provides faceted search over such information.
